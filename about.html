About Lqet Tech Solution (LTS)

LQET TECH SOLUTION (LTS) is a Technology company placed in Addis Ababa Ethiopia. We give basic computer skill, a web development, mobile app development and other technological services. LQET TECH SOLUTION (LTS) is in a critical development stage and with the physical and programmatic development of the technology the goal is becoming a globally collaborative and competitive technology academy which produces the highest quality academic and website developing center. To train computer technicians who have the knowledge and skills required by businesses about rapidly increasing computer technologies in our country and in the world.

Vission

Lqet Tech Solution aims to be ranked among the top ten pre-eminent African Bootcump in 2026.

Goal

We strive to be the best choice in the tech industry in the ethiopia; to create products of great quality and usefulness that contribute to the well-being of our society and guarantee the satisfaction of our clients, achieving long-term relationships.

Mission

Lqet Tech Solution’s mission is to give better academic based on the programs on which from basic computer skill to full stack web development (MERN).

As a leader in the tech industry, lqet tech solution believes that education is the best investment you can make in your future.

We’re committed to helping change lives and the world for the better. The astonishing speed of the developing tech industry is leaving individuals and societies alike in a struggle to cope.

People of all ages with aspirations in both tech and tech-related fields lack the skills to access some of the most productive and lucrative jobs out there, while our higher education systems simply can’t produce qualified personnel quickly enough to meet the ever-growing staff demands of the tech industry.

lqet tech solution was born to fix that problem. We turn learning tech skills into a process that is efficient, fast, and precisely targeted to meet the needs of both learners and employers today. Our bootcamps are designed for flexibility, offering both full-time and part-time options while combining online and on-site work
